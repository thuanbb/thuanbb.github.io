## Eigen Decomposition

$$ \mathbf{A} \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}$$, $$\mathbf{A}$$ is diagonalizable, then 

$$ \mathbf{A}= \mathbf{Q}\mathbf{\Lambda}\mathbf{Q}^{-1}$$, $$ \mathbf{Q} = \begin{bmatrix}
| & | & ...  & |\\
\mathbf{v}_{1} & \mathbf{v}_{2}  & ... & \mathbf{v}_{n}  \\ 
a| & | & ... & |
\end{bmatrix} $$
